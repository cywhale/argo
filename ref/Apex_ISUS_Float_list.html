5145MtyBay 9/17 to 9/21/2007 Monterey Bay
5145MtyBay
 A short test deployment of float 5145 to verify operation of ISUS. The float profiled twice each day for 5 days and was then recovered.
5145Hawaii (5901468)

12/22/2007 to 10/2011

 

 Hawaii Ocean Time series (HOT) station
5145Hawaii
 Planned four year deployment to assess long-term ISUS sensor performance. Low nitrate concentrations in surface waters near HOT will allow us to assess sensor drift over years. Float 5145 profiled once per day for the first 7 days and is now profiling every 5 days. The NO3 sensor stopped 9/2010 due to a software problem. The float was recovered 10/31/2011 to recalibrate the sensor.
5146SoOcean (5901492) 2/21/2008 to 3/2011 Southern Ocean on the CLIVAR I6S Line at 50 degrees S, which is southeast of Africa
5146SoOcean
 Seasonal consumption of nitrate during spring/summer phytoplankton blooms in the Southern Ocean tracks new primary production (NPP). Long-term measurements of nitrate will allow us to assess changes in NPP over interannual time scales.
